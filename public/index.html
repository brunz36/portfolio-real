Floridian

Growing up in area surrounded by the Gulf of Mexico, my hobbies have typically involved in one way or another the water. I started to fish the bay at a young age, and have been hooked ever since. These days you could find me on my kayak at Ft. DeSoto or Weedon Island Preserve fishing the dawn session.

Software Development

I enjoy learning and different programming languages are no different. I have recently focused on Ruby on Rails but I also studied some Python and Java, but I focused more on standalone GUI applications. My learning path will keep my busy with RoR but I also plan on spending some time on JS and React.

Motor Sports

As long as I remembered I was in awe of motorsport, in particular Formula One and its drivers. What attracted me to the sport was how a team of engineers blend science and art to create a machine for a singular purpose of achieving the best lap times. The technology they develop are the bleeding edge.
